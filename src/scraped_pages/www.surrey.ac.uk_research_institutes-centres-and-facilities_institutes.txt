Our research is supported by state-of-the-art research facilities and pan-University institutes. Surrey's research ecosystem is founded on pioneering, internationally recognised landmarks of disciplinary excellence through research. We continue to grow our research excellence through strategic partnerships with international industry, governments and academia.

The University has an impressive heritage in finding solutions to challenging real-world problems and supporting governments with the provision of evidence and policy guidance.

The primary purpose of our pan-University institutes is to support the University's aspirations for increasing the scale and enhancing the excellence of our research and innovation, working in close collaboration with our commercial and governmental partnership network. The institutes provide independent analysis and insight into key policy issues affecting our region, the UK and the world.

Our Institutes are already harnessing their interdisciplinary power to provide creative, collaborative and disruptive solutions and far-reaching insights and innovations for some of the world’s greatest challenges. Explore ourVision 2041 strategyto read more about our plans for future institutes.

Putting people at the heart of AI, augmenting human capabilities to deliver an inclusive and responsible force for good in partnership with industry, the public sector, government and national AI organisations.

We want to be a critical player in the global transition to sustainable living for everyone, through interdisciplinary research and innovation in partnership with national and international organisations, education and civic engagement.

Launched in 2025, the Surrey Space Institute harnesses more than forty years of Surrey satellite leadership to power bold, cross-disciplinary breakthroughs for today’s pressing challenges.
